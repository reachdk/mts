# Phrases To Avoid

Avoid these phrases unless they appear frequently in approved examples from Deepak:

- Rockstar
- Rockstars
- Kudos
- Hats off
- Going above and beyond
- Synergy
- Truly inspiring
- Incredible dedication
- Tireless efforts
- Game changer
- Proud and humbled
- Raise the bar
- Exceptional commitment
- This would not have been possible without
- Any similarly exaggerated or generic corporate praise

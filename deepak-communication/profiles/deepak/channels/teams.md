# Teams Channel Overlay

- Usually 70-150 words.
- No email greeting.
- No formal sign-off.
- Use first names.
- Informal but professional.
- Start directly with the initiative, result or reason for writing.
- Use two or three short paragraphs.
- End with a plain thank-you or acknowledgement.
- Suitable for posting in a shared Teams channel.
- Avoid making it sound like a formal company announcement.
